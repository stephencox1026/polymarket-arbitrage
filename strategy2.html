How It Works — Middle Bet Strategy

1. Different lines: When sportsbooks set different lines for the same prop (e.g. O/U 4.5 on DK vs O/U 5.5 on Poly), a "middle" opportunity may exist.

2. The bet: Bet Over on the lower line (e.g. Over 4.5) at one book, and Under on the higher line (e.g. Under 5.5) at another. Both bets cover every outcome, and they overlap in the middle (e.g. exactly 5).

3. Arb check: Convert each best odds to implied probability and sum them. If below 100%, you have a guaranteed profit on every outcome.

4. The middle bonus: If the result lands in the middle (e.g. exactly 5), both bets win — giving a much larger payout on top of the guaranteed profit.

Tip: The wider the gap between lines (e.g. 4.5 vs 6.5), the more "middle" values can hit. Even a 1-point gap (4.5 vs 5.5) gives a single middle value.
