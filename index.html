How It Works

1. Best line: For each outcome, pick the highest odds across the 3 books.

2. Arb check: Convert each best odds to implied probability (1 / odds) and sum them. If the sum is below 100%, an arb exists.

3. Stake split: Stake each outcome in proportion to its implied probability. The book with the best odds for that outcome is where you place it.

4. Profit: Guaranteed payout = total stake / sum. Profit = payout - total stake.

Tip: A 5%+ arb margin is ideal. Anything under 1-2% may not be worth the execution risk.
